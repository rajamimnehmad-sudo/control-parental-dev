DAG multilingual neural text model

The optional on-device text classifier derives its multilingual transformer from:

sentence-transformers/paraphrase-multilingual-MiniLM-L12-v2
https://huggingface.co/sentence-transformers/paraphrase-multilingual-MiniLM-L12-v2

The upstream model is distributed under the Apache License 2.0. DAG adds a
product-specific classification head and packages tokenization, inference and
post-processing into a quantized ONNX graph. The resulting model is downloaded
only from the pinned DEV artifact path and is accepted only after SHA-256
verification.

DAG professional local image safety model

Marqo/nsfw-image-detection-384
https://huggingface.co/Marqo/nsfw-image-detection-384

The bundled ONNX model is an INT8-weight-quantized ViT-Tiny image classifier trained by Marqo on a
proprietary 220,000-image dataset. The model card reports 98.56% accuracy on its
own balanced evaluation set. DAG uses it locally as the first member of a
conservative ensemble; the Yahoo OpenNSFW model remains a second independent
barrier. No image, score, or decision leaves the device.
